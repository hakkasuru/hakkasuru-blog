Tokyo was an incredible experience. The blend of ultra-modern technology with centuries-old temples creates a unique atmosphere you can't find anywhere else.

## Highlights

- **Shibuya Crossing** — The iconic scramble intersection at peak hour
- **Tsukiji Outer Market** — Fresh sushi for breakfast
- **Akihabara** — Electric Town in all its neon glory
- **Meiji Shrine** — A peaceful escape in the heart of the city
